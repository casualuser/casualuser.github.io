Coverage from **IEEE AIoT 2025** confirms that Artificial Intelligence of Things (AIoT) has moved beyond the proof-of-concept phase. The focus has shifted from "can we run AI on the edge?" to "how do we orchestrate it effectively?".

### Federated Learning
A key theme of the conference was the maturation of federated learning protocols. Devices can now collaboratively update global models without sharing raw user data, solving a major privacy bottleneck for healthcare and smart home applications.
