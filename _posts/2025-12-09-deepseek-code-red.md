### Efficiency Wins
This highlights a growing trend for 2025: the bifurcation of the market into massive reasoning models (like Opus 4.5) and highly efficient, domain-specific models (like DeepSeek). For DevOps teams, this validates the strategy of using smaller, specialized models for code generation tasks to optimize costs.
